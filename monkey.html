MONKEY

Monkeys are primates that belong to the suborder Haplorhini. They are known for their agility, intelligence, and diverse range of species.

Monkeys come in various shapes, sizes, and colors. They have long tails, flexible limbs, and grasping hands that allow them to climb, swing, and move through trees with great ease.

These playful creatures are highly social and live in groups called troops or bands. Troops can range in size from a few individuals to several dozen members, depending on the species. They communicate with each other using a combination of vocalizations, facial expressions, and body movements.
